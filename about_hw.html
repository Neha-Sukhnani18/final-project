Makai Bar & Restaurant

about us

Makai Bar & Restaurant in Lusaka, Zambia, is a vibrant, family-friendly spot at the Mulungushi Village Complex known for its multi-cuisine dishes (including Indian flavors), cocktails, and lively atmosphere with live DJs, karaoke, and events, offering indoor/outdoor seating, a kids' play area, and a pool, though some reviewers note potential service delays during busy times. It's ideal for relaxed dining, parties, or family outings, blending cultural decor with modern comforts and specials like Ladies' Nights and weekend events.

Location: Mulungushi Village Complex, Kalundu, Lusaka. Cuisine: Modern Fusion, Multi-Cuisine (Indian, general). Ambiance: Serene, vibrant, culturally themed with indoor (Zambian decor) & outdoor spaces, pool, and kids' play area. Features: Events (Karaoke, Football watch parties), DJs, Cocktails, Great Coffee, Family-Friendly. Good For: Families, groups, post-beach relaxation, parties, cultural experiences, tourists.
